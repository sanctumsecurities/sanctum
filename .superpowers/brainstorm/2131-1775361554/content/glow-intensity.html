Glow Intensity & Style

Which visual direction feels right for the charts and graphs?

A — Subtle / Professional

Clean lines, no glow filters. Colors as-is. Same palette, better contrast and fill opacity. Suits a financial terminal aesthetic.

B — Terminal Glow (Recommended)

SVG drop-shadow filters on lines and dots. Bars glow softly. Area fills get slight gradient. Feels like a Bloomberg/trading terminal. Same palette, luminous.

C — Neon / Cyberpunk

Heavy glow halos, brighter saturated colors (#93c5fd, #86efac). Bold and striking. Higher visual intensity — more gaming/crypto terminal energy.
